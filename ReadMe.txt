*** Definition ***
A social network, for movies. Share your opinio about movies with your friends.

*** How to run *** 
You need Python and Django to run the project. Project need some packages like simple Capcha.


*** Features ***
- Signup
- Login
- Follow other people to get their update in your timeline
- Rate a movie and share your point of view about it with your followers
- Like other users posts
- Comment on other users posts
- View other users profile / followers / following
- Page ination for timeline and user profiles
- Search other people / movies in the network
